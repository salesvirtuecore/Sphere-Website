What Happens Next

Your roadmap from here

1
Right Now
Check your email
You'll receive a confirmation with the date, time, and a Zoom or Google Meet link. Add it to your calendar so you don't forget.

2
Before the Call
Spend 5 minutes preparing
Think about which topics feel hardest, what your current or predicted grade is, and what you're aiming for. No formal prep needed — just a rough picture in your head.
3
On the Call
20 minutes with Harrison
He'll ask about your situation, identify the specific gaps costing you marks, and walk you through what the first few sessions would look like. You'll leave knowing exactly what to work on.
💡 Join from a quiet space. You don't need to bring any materials to this first call.
4
After the Call
Book your first session (optional)
If it feels like a good fit, Harrison will send a payment link for your chosen package. Sessions are booked weekly at a time that suits you.
